def safe_access(container, i):
    result = container.split('|')
    try:
        return result[i]
    except IndexError or Ksafe_accesseyError:
        return pd.np.nan

Conclusions¶

In conclusion, the movie industry is booming in term of finance and profitability. The first question asks about the volume of projects in Hollywood and how it changes over time, and the chart shows how the number of movies produced every year is increacing. In the part of the second question I am trying to get an estimation of the cost of creating a movie and how it changes over the years. Since the 2000s the cost of making movies has decline which might explian the rise of the number of movies since the year 2000. Which genre generates more money, the third question found that Action, Adventure, and fantasy movies are the most profitable among genres, while genres like Foreign movies and documentaries have scored the lowest in terms of revenue and profitability. Which give us an idea what kind movies are making more money.
